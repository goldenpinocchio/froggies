Frogies, but flatter.

This direction leans into flat vector illustration, minimal digital art, mid-century calm, and simple geometric shapes. It keeps the frog charm, but lets each trait swap cleanly without losing the composition.

Why this fits the reference

Soft pastel fills, almost no texture, simplified geometry, roomy composition, and a calm nature scene. The frog can still express rarity through trait changes — gold, laser, bubbles, crowns, and color variants — without breaking the illustration language.

This is a working style direction, not the final lock. If this feels right, the next step is to bring the rest of the collection into this same flat editorial language.
