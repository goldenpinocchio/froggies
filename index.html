Frogies

RARITY DIRECTION
- Common = soft greens, sleepy faces, simple accessories
- Uncommon = pastel variants, more personality
- Rare = gold eyes, crowns, stronger glow, more dramatic props
- Ultra-rare = gold body, diamond body, laser eyes, top-tier aura effects

NOTES
- The block data should decide which traits appear
- Rare traits should be driven by rarer deterministic patterns
- Gold / diamond should be much less common than green bodies
- Laser eyes should be special, not frequent
- The whole collection should still feel cute even at the rare end

Process notes

The collection is intended to follow the Natcats spirit: a deterministic system where the Bitcoin block data decides the outcome, while the trait set itself stays curated and easy to reason about.
